introduction

The information technology industry is one of the most important industries in our country. As the department of information of the only top university in Central my country, the department should focus on cultivating talents in the national cutting-edge information industry. In order to cultivate all-round information talents with information theory, hardware, software, network multimedia and information application, the curriculum planning of this department focuses on the following five areas: network multimedia, embedded systems, intelligent systems, Internet of Things and Cloud computing and information security, and have the following school-running features: Curriculum planning emphasizes both theoretical and practical courses, such as: "Logic Design", "Operating System", "Electronic Circuits" and "Logic Design Experiment", "Operating System Experiment" and "Electronic Circuit Experiment" are set up together; "Information topic" is listed as a compulsory course to cultivate information talents with theoretical foundation and practical ability.

Educational goals

  1. Cultivate information talents with expertise in information theory, hardware, software, network multimedia and information applications.

  2. Cultivate information talents with theoretical foundation and practical ability.

  3. Cultivate talents with self-challenge and lifelong learning ability.

Undergraduate core competence

  1. Possess information science literacy, information theory and mathematical analysis capabilities.

  2. Have the ability to analyze, design and implement information hardware systems.

  3. Have the ability to analyze, design and implement information software systems.

  4. Have the ability to analyze, design and integrate information application systems.

  5. Have the ability to analyze, design and implement information networks and multimedia systems.

  6. Possesses the ability of self-learning, communication and coordination and teamwork.
